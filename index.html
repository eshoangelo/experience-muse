At Experience Muse, we believe coffee is more than a drink - it's an experience. Nestled in Sterling Heights' County Line Plaza, our cozy space is where craft meets community.

From our viral Cloud drinks to perfectly poured lattes, every cup is made with care and creativity. Come for the coffee, stay for the vibes.
